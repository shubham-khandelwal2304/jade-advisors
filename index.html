Jade Advisors is a leading placement agent platform focusing on providing capital raising and advisory services to alternative asset managers worldwide.

Jade Advisors focusses on mandates where it has a deep investor network. All client engagements are handled directly by Jades’ senior team members, providing an efficient route to the asset manager to expand their investor base in a targeted manner. The team actively engages throughout the entire fund raising process, starting from refining the fund positioning strategy and marketing material preparations, to investors filtrations and roadshows and perform all the required coordination until successfully achieving the desired outcomes for the clients.
