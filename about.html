About

Established in 2005 by Peter Jädersten and Björn Klarin with a focus on the Middle Eastern region, the firm now has expanded to a large number of senior teams with presence across some of the key fund raising markets globally. Over the years, the firm has developed a diverse client base comprising of prominent fund managers with varied AUM across Europe, United States, Latin America, Africa and Asia. Jade Advisors concentrates its fund raising efforts on a broad range of investors, including Sovereign Wealth Funds, Pension Funds, Funds-of-funds, Institutions, Large and mid-sized Family offices, Consultants/Gate-Keepers and Asset Management Companies.
